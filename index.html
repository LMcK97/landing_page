Types of Rock

Marble, a metamorphic rock composed of recrystallized minerals.
Slate is a fine-grained, foliated metamorphic rock that is created by the alteration of shale or mudstone by low-grade regional metamorphism.
Granite forms from magma with a high content of silica and alkali metal oxides that slowly cools and solidifies underground.
